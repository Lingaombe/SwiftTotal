# **Design Brief (GoodBrief)**
**Company Name:** <br>
Swift Total

**Company Description:** <br>
We are a company that makes and distributes board games. They stand out because they are cheap and available in stores worldwide. Our target audience is women. We want to convey a sense of faithfulness, while at the same time being old-fashioned.

**Job Description:** <br>
You must create a website that will mainly provide information. The goal is to make it clear what the company does. Besides the landing page, the website will need a information page, product pages and a blog. The landing page should have a Our Values section. There should be a call to action to get users to subscribe to the newsletter. They would prefer a colorful design, and would like you to use the brand color, which is red. Take into account the client's preferences and values.

**Deadline:** <br>
5 days
